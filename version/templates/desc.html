Scrim 1

All the queries regarding the event will be addressed at our Version'21 Community Server .

The Scrims are being conducted by Team Version.

This year we bring the very first edition of the Scrims Initiative- Scrim 1.

This contest will check your problem-solving skills.

Participate and outsmart other participants to reach at the peak of the Leaderboard.

May the best coder win!

Starts at 08:00 PM, 12 June 2021

  • This is an open coding contest, hosted on hackerearth.
  • There will be 8 problems and you will get 2 hours and 30 minutes to solve them.
  • Points are distributed according to problem levels.
  • The Contest is open i.e. it will not be proctored.
  • At the end of the Contest, participants having maximum scores will win.
  • It is an individual event.
  • Doubts regarding anything should be asked directly to the contacts provided by Team Version. Posting it in public forums will not be entertained.
  • Unfair means to guarantee advantage will lead to disqualification.
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • The solutions should not be made public or open-sourced during the contest.
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

Judging Criteria: At the end of the contest, the participants having highest score on the leaderboard win.
