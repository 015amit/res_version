Dappnovation

All the queries regarding the event will be addressed at our Version'21 Community Server .

Dappnovation is a DApps based hackathon.

It is a team event where we expect you to come up with creative and innovative solutions to everyday problems in the form of a decentralized application.

There are couple of examples you can choose from – Online Voting System, Healthcare System, Social Media.

You are not limited to these domains only. You can pick up any domain of your choice given that the final product is in the form of a decentralized application.

The Dappnovation will be in three phases:

  1. Abstract Submission along with the registration which start tomorrow and the last date for the same is 15th August.
  2. In the 2nd phase, there will be two review rounds in a space of 7-10 days.
    • In both the review rounds, the teams must present their projects in front of a panel of judges. They will also be questioned about their projects and the technologies used. The top teams will then advance to the next phase.
    • The 1st review round will be on 29th August, and we expect the teams must have completed at least 60% of their project.
    • By the 2nd review round, your projects must be close to completion. The exact date for the 2nd review round hasn’t been finalized yet. But rest assured there will at least be a gap of 1 week between both the rounds. The teams will be updated as soon as the dates are finalized, and you can also check our website for regular updates.
  3. The 3rd phase is the final round where the winners of Dappnovation will be declared.
  • Your project must be a decentralized application. You can select any domain of your choice.
  • The teams can be of 3-5 members. The students can be studying in different years of MCA; however, they must be from the same college. A thorough verification will be done of every team before the distribution of prizes.
  • Any form of unfair practice will lead to immediate disqualification.
