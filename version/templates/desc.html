CTB

All the queries regarding the event will be addressed at our Version'21 Community Server .

Capture the block (CTB) contest is a special kind of cybersecurity competition designed to challenge its participants to solve computer security and blockchain problems and/or capture and defend computer systems.

The game consists of a series of challenges where participants must reverse engineer, break, hack, decrypt, or do whatever it takes to solve the challenge.

The challenges are all set up with the intent of being hacked, making it an excellent, legal way to get hands-on experience.

  • This is a 2-day continuous event.
  • CTB touches on many other aspects of information security: blockchain, cryptography, stego, binary analysis, reverse engineering, forensics, and others.
  • Challenges are divided into categories and each category will have three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.
  • At the end of the Contest, teams from different colleges having maximum scores will win.
  • In case of a tie, different parameters like no. of members in the team, the total number of wrong attempts, flags submission timestamp, etc. will be considered.

Participation Rules:

  • The contest is open only for MCA students of India.
  • Students can participate as an individual or in a team. Every participant must be a part of a team (create an individual team in case you are playing alone) for being visible on the hacker board.
  • All the members of a team must belong to the same institute.
  • Participants have to leave (or disband in case of captain) the existing team before joining the new one.
  • Only the top scorer team of every college will make it to the hacker board.

General Rules and Instructions :

  • Each question has some points to it depending upon the difficulty.
  • There is no particular order of solving the questions.
  • Ranks can be viewed on the hacker board page. The leaderboard time is updated every time a submission is made.
  • Flags found are of the format ctb{some_text}. Some of the files may contain just the inner text within the brackets. Make sure you submit it in the format specified.
  • Internet access is granted. Feel free to explore and read about the concept behind the problem.

Special Rules and Instructions :

  • Don't try to hack anything apart from this CTB Network.
  • Don't try to hack other members.
  • Don't spoil! Don't share how you solved each challenge with other members.
  • Performing denial of service attacks on the server will lead to disqualification. You are requested to play ethically.
  • Brute force attacks on the flag form are prohibited.
