Scrim 1

The Scrim is initiated and being conducted by Version 21.

And this year, Version 21 presents you with the very first edition of the Scrim Initiative- Scrim 1.

This contest will check your problem-solving skill.

Participate and outsmart the other participants to reach at the peak of the Leaderboard.

May the best coder win!

Starts at 08:00 PM, 12 June 2021

  • This is an open coding contest, hosted on hackerearth.
  • There will be 8 problems, and you will get 2 hours and 30 minutes to solve them.
  • Points are distributed according to problem level.
  • The Contest is open i.e. it will not be proctored.
  • At the end of Contest, participants having maximum points will win.
  • It is an individual event.
  • Doubts regarding anything should be asked directly to the event coordinators only. Posting it in public forums will not be entertained.
  • Unfair means to guarantee advantage will lead to disqualification.
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • The solutions should not be made public or open-sourced during the contest.
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

Judging Criteria: At the end of the contest, the participants having highest score on the leaderboard wins. Scores will be aggregated based upon the results of both the rounds.
